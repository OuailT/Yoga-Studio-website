WANDERLUST’S 200-HOUR POWER
TEACHER TRAINING

Join Wanderlust Yoga this Fall for our 200-hour Power Yoga Teacher Training online! You will explore the ancient lineage and philosophy of Yoga, learn and experience the elements of Power Yoga, and discover how this practice will empower you on and off your mat.

This 3-month program will teach both aspiring teachers and Power Yoga practitioners how to create and sequence a powerful yoga class, to strengthen leadership and communication skills for teaching and connecting with others, and to live a life in alignment with your True North.

All trainees get 3 months of on-demand yoga classes from Wanderlust Yoga's online library!

This training consists of the following key elements:

Techniques in how to teach and practice the elements of Power Yoga

Studies of pranayama and meditation

Self-inquiry and self-discovery work

The business of yoga, personal branding, and marketing

The power of feedback: How to give and receive

Practice teaching with group and one-on-one coaching

Anatomy of Power Yoga

The subtle body: Chakras, Nadis and Koshas

The 5-elements

The history of Power Yoga and yoga philosophy, including Patanjali’s Yoga Sutras and the 8-Limbs of Yoga

Tools to create a yoga class, including theme, sequence, and music

This is a 200-hour Registered Teacher Training with Yoga Alliance.
